Playthrough #1

The party made introductions in a Caravan after hearing about Sildar Hallwinters’ request to help him find his daughter. Wagoning down to Phandalin to meet up with Gundren Rockseeker, a friend of Sildar’s who supposedly took his granddaughter, Austiria without explanation. Along the way, the party was ambushed by a militia of Goblins. It was a harsh fight, but the party left victorious after an inexplicable event happened as one of the goblins transformed into an unknown creature but inevitably self-imploding. The events worked out for the party but caused two remaining goblins to run away towards the mountains. The wagon suffered minimal damage but the bison that were pulling the wagon escaped. The party collectively goes to gather the bison from the surrounding area and as they do, they are stunned to see a luminous woman as if she is on fire. They make contact, discovering their presence, the woman locks eyes, screams, and ignites a huge fire while the surrounding area turns completely black and all that remain are a pair of two menacing eyes in the sky. The woman disappeared, leaving a burned spot on the ground, destroying a nearby garden of flowers. The party retrieves the Bison and returns to the wagon. Sildar discovers the infamous goblin trail is nearby the wagon and suggests everyone come along and follow it for their might be some clue as to what happened with Gundren and Austuria.
