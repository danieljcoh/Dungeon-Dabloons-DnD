The world as we know it is changing. The human realm, no longer human. No longer homogenous. With the collapse of the other realms and the genocide of the gods, those races have no where to go but to the human realm. We were told that the gods all agreed that killing themselves was the best thing for creation... but is that true? Is that the truth? With the joining of different races came multiple tensions and conflicts and the last remaining gods on the human realm made it so fighting was outlawed against other races. So what did humans do? We became our own gods and made our own creations to wage for us.
